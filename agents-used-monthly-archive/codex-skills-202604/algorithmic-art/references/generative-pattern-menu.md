# Generative Pattern Menu

Choose one primary family before coding.

## Flow Fields

Use when the piece should feel like wind, currents, migration, or invisible force.

Good knobs:
- noise scale
- particle count
- step size
- trail decay

## Particle Systems

Use when the piece needs birth, motion, death, clustering, or swarm behavior.

Good knobs:
- emitter count
- lifetime
- drag
- spawn rate

## Oscillator Systems

Use when the piece is about rhythm, interference, breathing, or wave superposition.

Good knobs:
- frequency
- phase offset
- amplitude
- coupling

## Cellular Or Rule Systems

Use when the piece should evolve from local rules into large structure.

Good knobs:
- neighborhood rule
- mutation rate
- update cadence

## Packing And Growth

Use when the piece is about crowding, expansion, territory, or pressure.

Good knobs:
- growth rate
- collision margin
- spawn strategy

## Recursive Geometry

Use when the piece needs branching, self-similarity, or architectural order.

Good knobs:
- depth
- angle variance
- shrink factor

## Noise-Driven Terrain Or Ink

Use when the piece should feel geological, cloudy, watery, or organic.

Good knobs:
- octave count
- persistence
- contour threshold

## Selection Rule

Pick the family that matches the motion logic first.
Palette, line style, and layering should support that motion, not rescue a weak system.
