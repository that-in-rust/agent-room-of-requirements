**Counterexample, gotchas, and operational comparison.** Common errors are ceremony without insight, theatrical experts, hidden reasoning requests, decorative novelty, a literature dump, false certainty, and verification that leaves the draft unchanged. Good: test a platform migration through operator, economist, user, domain, and skeptical lenses, compare four paths, then revise after claim checks. Bad: invent prestigious personas and endorse the first option. Borderline: a simple factual lookup needs source checking but not a council.

**Verification, evidence, and uncertainty.** Audit the calibration fields, lens rationale, conventional and alternative paths, evidence labels, skeptical challenge, three-to-seven key claim checks, resulting revisions, final thesis, and open questions.
